Our Services

Professional truck and trailer services to keep your fleet running smoothly.

Inspections & Compliance

Clean Truck Check

Comprehensive pre-trip and post-trip vehicle inspections to ensure your truck meets all safety and compliance standards. Our certified technicians perform thorough checks of all critical systems including brakes, lights, tires, and safety equipment. We provide detailed inspection reports to help you maintain compliance and identify potential issues before they become costly problems.

BIT Inspections

Brake, Inspection, and Tire (BIT) inspections to ensure your vehicle meets all regulatory requirements. Our certified inspectors examine brake systems, conduct comprehensive vehicle inspections, and evaluate tire condition and tread depth. BIT inspections are essential for maintaining DOT compliance and ensuring the safety of your commercial vehicle operations.

DOT Inspection

Certified Department of Transportation (DOT) inspections to keep your fleet compliant with federal and state regulations. We perform Level I through Level V inspections covering all critical safety systems. Our certified inspectors ensure your vehicles meet FMCSA standards, helping you avoid violations and keep your fleet on the road legally and safely.

Maintenance & Service Programs

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance programs designed to prevent breakdowns and extend the life of your equipment. Our comprehensive PM programs include oil changes, filter replacements, fluid checks, belt and hose inspections, and system diagnostics. We create customized maintenance schedules based on your fleet's specific needs, helping you reduce unexpected downtime and costly repairs while maximizing vehicle reliability and resale value.

Mobile Truck Repair

On-site mobile repair services that come to you, saving time and minimizing downtime. Our fully equipped service trucks are staffed with experienced diesel technicians ready to handle repairs at your location, whether you're at a loading dock, rest area, or breakdown site. We provide diagnostic services, emergency repairs, and routine maintenance on-site, getting you back on the road faster without the need to tow your vehicle.

Emergency Services

Roadside Assistance

24/7 emergency roadside assistance to get you back on the road quickly, no matter where you are. Our rapid response teams provide tire changes, jump starts, fuel delivery, lockout service, and minor repairs at the roadside. Available throughout New Jersey and New York, we understand that time is money in the trucking industry, and we're committed to minimizing your downtime with fast, professional service.

Truck Towing

Professional heavy-duty towing services for trucks and trailers of all sizes. Our fleet of specialized tow trucks can handle everything from light-duty commercial vehicles to Class 8 trucks and fully loaded trailers. We provide safe, efficient towing to our service facility or your preferred location, with careful attention to protecting your vehicle and cargo during transport.

Specialized Vehicle Services

Trailer Repair

Expert repair services for all trailer types including dry van, flatbed, reefer, and specialty trailers. Our technicians handle everything from structural repairs and door maintenance to electrical systems, brake repairs, and suspension work. We understand that trailer downtime directly impacts your revenue, so we prioritize quick turnaround times while maintaining the highest quality standards for all trailer repair work.

General Diesel Repair

Complete diesel engine repair and maintenance for all makes and models of commercial trucks. Our experienced diesel technicians diagnose and repair engine issues, fuel systems, cooling systems, and all related components. We work on all major diesel engine manufacturers and stay current with the latest diagnostic equipment and repair techniques to ensure your diesel engine performs at peak efficiency.

Bus Repair

Specialized repair services for commercial buses, school buses, and transit vehicles. We understand the unique requirements and regulations governing bus operations, including passenger safety standards and emissions compliance. Our bus repair services cover engine work, transmission repairs, HVAC systems, electrical diagnostics, and all mechanical systems critical to safe passenger transportation.

RV Repair

Comprehensive repair and maintenance services for recreational vehicles and motorhomes. We service both the chassis and coach portions of RVs, handling diesel engine repairs, generator service, HVAC systems, plumbing, electrical systems, and all mechanical components. Whether you're a full-time RVer or weekend traveler, we keep your home on wheels running smoothly and safely.

Parts & Support

Truck Parts

Quality replacement parts and components for all major truck manufacturers including Freightliner, Peterbilt, Kenworth, Volvo, Mack, International, and more. We maintain an extensive inventory of OEM and aftermarket parts to minimize wait times and keep your repairs on schedule. Our parts specialists help you find the right components for your specific vehicle, ensuring proper fit and optimal performance.
