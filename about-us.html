Who We Are

S5 Truck Repair is a Sikh-owned, family-operated business and a leading provider of heavy-duty truck and trailer repair services across New Jersey and New York. Based in Flemington, New Jersey, we've built our reputation on fast response times, honest pricing, and expert craftsmanship.

Our team of ASE-certified diesel mechanics brings decades of combined experience to every job. Whether you're an owner-operator stranded on the highway or a fleet manager needing scheduled maintenance, we're here to keep your trucks running and your business moving forward.

We understand that downtime costs money. That's why we offer 24/7 emergency roadside assistance with average dispatch times under 45 minutes across our entire service area. Our mobile service units are equipped to handle everything from simple tire changes to complex engine diagnostics, bringing the shop to you when you need it most.
